# Vision: Asteroid Field

## North Star
A browser-playable retro space game that captures the tension of navigating a dense asteroid field. Simple controls, pixel art aesthetic, escalating difficulty. Playable in 30 seconds, addictive for 30 minutes.

## Audience
- Casual gamers looking for a quick session
- Retro game enthusiasts
- Visitors to neenhouse.com exploring the demo grid

## Design Principles
1. **Instant playability** — no tutorials, no loading screens. WASD + Space.
2. **Retro aesthetic** — low-res pixel art, limited palette, space atmosphere
3. **Escalating challenge** — starts calm, gets chaotic
4. **Browser-native** — Canvas 2D, no plugins, runs everywhere
