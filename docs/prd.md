# Product Requirements: Asteroid Field

## Features

| # | Feature | Status | Description |
|---|---------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Ship movement | PLANNED | WASD/arrow key movement, momentum-based physics |
| 2 | Shooting | PLANNED | Spacebar fires projectiles, limited fire rate |
| 3 | Asteroid spawning | PLANNED | Asteroids drift from edges, varying sizes and speeds |
| 4 | Collision & breaking | PLANNED | Asteroids break into smaller pieces when shot, ship explodes on contact |
| 5 | Score & difficulty | PLANNED | Score increases with destroyed asteroids, spawn rate escalates over time |
| 6 | Game over & restart | PLANNED | Death screen with score, press Enter to restart |
| 7 | Pixel art rendering | PLANNED | Low-res canvas upscaled with image-rendering: pixelated, retro palette |
